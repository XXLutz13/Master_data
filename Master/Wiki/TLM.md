
## transfer length method

### contact resistivity:

$\rho_C =\frac{\Delta_V}{J}=\frac{(V_{Semiconductor}-V_{Metal})\cdot A}{I}$

### Total resistance measured by TLM:
$R_{tot}=\frac{R_S}{Z}d+2R_C$

Where the first component is the sheet resistance and the second the contact resistance

### Transfer length:
$L_T=\sqrt{\frac{\rho_C}{R_S}}$
